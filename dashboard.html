Similarity score guide
1.0 — identical genre mix
0.7–0.9 — strong genre overlap
0.4–0.6 — partial match
0.1–0.3 — loose similarity
How it works

Genres are converted to TF-IDF vectors. Cosine similarity measures the angle between them — 1.0 means parallel (same genres), 0 means no shared genres at all.
